About Us

Our journey with coffee began in the heart of the Blue Mountains, where we discovered the perfect blend of passion and craftsmanship.

Founded in 1990, our coffee shop is dedicated to delivering the best quality coffee experience to our customers. Every cup is a testament to our commitment to excellence, authenticity, and community. Our beans are ethically sourced and carefully crafted to perfection.

We believe in more than just coffee; we believe in the power of bringing people together and creating moments that matter. Come visit us and be part of our story.

Our Mission

Crafting the finest coffee, ethically sourced, expertly roasted.

We are committed to providing sustainable and environmentally friendly coffee, while ensuring our farmers receive fair wages and conditions. Our mission is to inspire coffee enthusiasts with each cup, creating a sustainable future and a community that thrives on quality, creativity, and passion.
